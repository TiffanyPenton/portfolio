Preventive Maintenance Tracker

Web-based maintenance tracking tool built to organize recurring service tasks, log completed work, and provide a practical view of equipment upkeep over time.

Overview

This project focused on building a lightweight digital tool for organizing maintenance activity, tracking service intervals, and making recurring upkeep easier to manage in a clear and practical way.

Problem

Maintenance information can easily become inconsistent or difficult to track when it is spread across notes, memory, or disconnected records. I wanted a cleaner way to log service activity, monitor schedules, and keep maintenance work visible over time.

What I Built

I built a simple web-based tracking tool designed to organize maintenance-related information in one place, support recurring maintenance schedules, and present the data in a way that is easy to review and update.

Outcome

The result was a practical, maintainable tool that supports better recordkeeping and helps turn routine maintenance into a clearer, more manageable process.
