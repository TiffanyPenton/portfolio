XBox 1 Controller Rebuild

Hands-on repair project focused on diagnosis, disassembly, component-level troubleshooting, and restoring reliable function.

Overview

This project focused on rebuilding an XBox 1 controller by diagnosing faults, taking the unit apart, identifying worn or failed components, and working through the repair process to restore reliable operation.

Problem

The controller required repair and rebuild work to return it to dependable working condition. The project involved understanding the hardware, identifying the source of the issue, and deciding what needed to be repaired, replaced, or reassembled.

What I Did

I disassembled the controller, inspected the internal components, worked through the repair process, and rebuilt the unit with the goal of restoring stable function. The project required patience, close observation, and a methodical troubleshooting approach.

Outcome

This project highlights my hands-on repair skills, my ability to break down a system into smaller problem areas, and my focus on practical troubleshooting rather than guesswork.
